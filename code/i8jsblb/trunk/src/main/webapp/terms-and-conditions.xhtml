  1. The main security feature of the Service is the four (4) digits transaction pin, which is used to authorize every JCash transaction requests.
  2. For certain transactions, a unique transaction reference number shall be generated, which will remain activate for a specific time period before completion & authorization, after which it will not be recognized by the system.
  3. The security of the transaction PIN is the customer's responsibility, which may not be compromised under any circumstances. The customer must not store the PIN in his/her mobile handset in any way whatsoever and must not disclose it to anyone including JS Bank limited.
  4. If anyone gains access to the customer's registered JCash account number along with his/her PIN and SIM, he/she can authorize all JCash transactions on the customer's behalf. The customer will be liable for any loss caused by the transactions processed during this period until the customer advice the Bank to suspend or block the service.
  5. In case of lost/stolen handset or SIM, the customer must inform immediately at JCash Help Line "170" for blocking of his/her Registered JCash Account.
  6. Upon a SIM replacement for the same number, the transaction PIN shall be changed. The customer will be required to call the Helpline "170" and request for PIN change before the usage of JCash account.
